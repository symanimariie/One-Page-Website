My Favorite Tv Show

K.C. Undercover is a Disney Channel sitcom about a high school student named K.C. Cooper who discovers her parents are secret spies and recruits her to join their family-run spy organization. Along with her parents and younger siblings, including a sarcastic robot sister named Judy, K.C. balances normal teenage life with dangerous missions to save the world while keeping her secret identity from her best friend, Marisa. The show follows the Cooper family as they deal with both everyday issues and their high-stakes undercover work.
